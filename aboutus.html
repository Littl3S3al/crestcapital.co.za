WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Larger organisations tend to enlist the services of investment banks to guide them through the corporate finance transaction process. The mid-sized to smaller listed company and privately owned businesses entering a complex corporate finance transaction are mostly left to fend for themselves or rely on their auditors or tax practitioners for help. Unfortunately M&A transactions are not these advisors main focus and, whilst they may have had involvement in such work from time to time, it is certainly not frequent enough for them to be considered experts in the complex world of corporate finance. Equally, as business owners who have experienced the delights and pit-falls of business, we know what it takes to conclude a successful transaction or reorganisation. We are well qualified to walk the path with you to ensure you continue on a successful journey.
